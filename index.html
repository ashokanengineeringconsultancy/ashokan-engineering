About Us

Built on Real Project Experience

Ashokan Engineering Consultancy Ltd is a UK-registered engineering consultancy founded by Vishal Ashokan, a Senior AVEVA E3D/PDMS Administrator and Piping Designer with over 12 years of hands-on EPC project experience.

Having delivered complex engineering solutions on major offshore and onshore projects including Valhall PWP, Hugin B, and Norfolk Vanguard at Aker Solutions, we bring the technical depth and project discipline of a major EPC house — at the agility and value of an independent consultancy.

We combine deep AVEVA platform expertise with modern data engineering skills — Python, PostgreSQL, Power BI — enabling us to bridge the gap between traditional plant design and digital engineering, offering clients a genuinely comprehensive service package.
