About Us

Built on Real Project Experience

Ashokan Engineering Consultancy Ltd is a UK-registered engineering consultancy founded by Vishal Ashokan, a Senior AVEVA E3D Administrator and Senior Piping Designer with over 12 years of hands-on EPC project experience.

At Aker Solutions, Vishal served as AVEVA E3D Administrator and CATS & SPECS Specialist covering Piping, Structure, Electrical and HVAC disciplines — alongside his role as Senior Piping Designer on major offshore projects including Valhall PWP, Hugin B, and Norfolk Vanguard.

We combine deep AVEVA platform expertise with modern data engineering skills — Python, PostgreSQL, Power BI — enabling us to bridge the gap between traditional plant design and digital engineering, offering clients a genuinely comprehensive service package.
